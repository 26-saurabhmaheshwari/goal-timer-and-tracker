How the numbers work

Plain-English definition of every figure on Overview, Pot and Time Planning. All figures recompute live each time you open a page.

Source of truth

Everything is derived from two raw inputs stored on this device (and synced via Firebase):

  • Logged entries — every time-log you record. This is the only source of Done hours.
  • Goals & Habits config — your targets and daily habits. This is the only source of To do hours.

There is no separate stored "total". Overview, Pot and Time Planning all call one function (planYearTotals()), so their headline numbers are always identical — change a goal or log time and all three move together.

Done

Sum of every logged entry this year, by category. Counts all logged time regardless of a goal's status — time on backlog or dream goals still adds to Done.

To do (a.k.a. "left")

The forward commitment for the rest of the year:

  • Active goals only (backlog & dream excluded). A normal goal's remaining = (target − current) × minutes-per-unit. A recurring goal = per-period × periods left.
  • Daily habits = sum(habit minutes/day) × days left in the year.

Total / Capacity

Total = Done + To do. This is the Pot's capacity and the Overview battery's total. It is not 3h/day × 365 — it reflects your actual plan.

Per period · Per month · Per day

  • A period = a third of a month: P1 = days 1–10, P2 = 11–20, P3 = 21–end (~3 periods/month).
  • Per period = To do ÷ periods left in the year.
  • Per month = To do ÷ months left (days left ÷ 30.44).
  • Per day required = To do ÷ days left, shown split as fixed (your daily habits — a constant) + goals (the remainder from active goals).

Consistency note

Overview (year), Pot and Time Planning share the same source (Time Planning), so Total / Done / To do match exactly across them. The monthly view shows only the category donut + breakdown for each month — actual logged time, no targets.
