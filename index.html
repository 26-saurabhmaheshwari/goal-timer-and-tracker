Welcome 👋

A paper-first tracker built around one small ritual: every 10 days, sit down once and log where your time and effort actually went. No timers, no daily nagging.
1. Open Settings → set your name and rename the starter categories (Health, Learning, Work…) to fit your life.
2. Add a few things you're working toward in Goals.
3. Every 10 days, come back and log your period. That's the whole habit.

How the numbers work

Plain-English definition of every figure on Overview (its year and Pot views) and Time Planning. All figures recompute live each time you open a page.

Source of truth

Everything is derived from two raw inputs stored on this device (and synced via Firebase):

  • Logged entries — every time-log you record. This is the only source of Done hours.
  • Goals & Habits config — your targets and daily habits. This is the only source of To do hours.

There is no separate stored "total". Overview, Pot and Time Planning all call one function (planYearTotals()), so their headline numbers are always identical — change a goal or log time and all three move together.

Done

Sum of every logged entry this year, by category. Counts all logged time regardless of a goal's status — time on someday goals still adds to Done.

To do (a.k.a. "left")

The forward commitment for the rest of the year:

  • Live Nuggets only (Quarterly, Yearly, Someday and completed goals excluded). A goal's remaining = (target − current) × minutes-per-unit.
  • Daily habits = sum(habit minutes/day) × days left in the year.

Total / Capacity

Total = Done + To do. This is the Pot's capacity. It is not 3h/day × 365 — it reflects your actual plan.

Per period · Per month · Per day

  • A period = a third of a month: P1 = days 1–10, P2 = 11–20, P3 = 21–end (~3 periods/month).
  • Per period = To do ÷ periods left in the year.
  • Per month = To do ÷ months left (days left ÷ 30.44).
  • Per day required = To do ÷ days left, shown split as fixed (your daily habits — a constant) + goals (the remainder from active goals).

"Days left" always means the real days between today and 31 Dec — that is what every per-period/month/day figure divides by. The planning window you set in Settings (default 210 days) only changes the date strip and "days planned" label on Time Planning; it does not change the pace math.

Consistency note

Overview (year and Pot views) and Time Planning share the same source, so Total / Done / To do match exactly across them. The monthly view shows only the category donut + breakdown for each month — actual logged time, no targets.
